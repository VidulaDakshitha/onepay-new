Additional Fees

Setup Fees

Onepay Payment Gateway setup fees would be LKR 1,500. Once your document approved by the Bank you will have to pay before going live.

Premium Card-Brand Fees

American Express, Discover & Diners Club, or any other premium cards there will be additional fee above the standard Payment Processing Fees as follows;

Standard Plan 3.6%

Essential Plan 3.5%

Elevate plan 3.5%

Premier plan 3.5%

Cross-Border Foreign Currency Fees

Accepting payments in foreign currencies will incur a 1.5% additional fee above the standard Payment Processing Fee of your active plan. Amount will be converted to LKR at bank exchange rate & settled in LKR.

Other payment options

LankaQR would be 0.5% for all plans. For the other methods standard Payment Processing Fee of your active plan.
